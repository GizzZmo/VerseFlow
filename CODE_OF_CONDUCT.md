VerseFlow is dedicated to fostering an inclusive, welcoming, and collaborative environment for all members of the independent music community. ## üåü Our Standards

### Positive Behavior ‚úÖ

We encourage behavior that contributes to a positive and welcoming environment:

- **Be respectful**: Treat all community members with respect and kindness
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and help them feel valued
- **Be collaborative**: Share knowledge and help others learn
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be patient**: Remember that everyone has different skill levels and backgrounds
- **Be open-minded**: Consider different perspectives and approaches
- **Celebrate diversity**: Embrace different musical styles, cultural backgrounds, and creative approaches

### Music Community Values üé∂

- **Support independent artists**: Encourage and uplift emerging talent
- **Respect creative work**: Acknowledge and credit artistic contributions
- **Foster collaboration**: Help connect artists, producers, and creators
- **Share knowledge**: Help others improve their musical and technical skills
- **Build bridges**: Connect different music communities and genres

### Unacceptable Behavior ‚ùå

The following behaviors are not tolerated in our community:

- **Harassment**: Unwelcome comments, threats, or intimidation
- **Discrimination**: Prejudicial treatment based on personal characteristics
- **Trolling**: Deliberately provocative or disruptive behavior
- **Spam**: Excessive self-promotion or irrelevant content
- **Doxxing**: Publishing private information without consent
- **Sexual harassment**: Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- **Hate speech**: Content that attacks or demeans individuals or groups
- **Copyright violation**: Sharing copyrighted material without permission
- **Impersonation**: Falsely representing yourself as another person or entity

## üéØ Guidelines for Specific Interactions

### Code Reviews
- Focus on the code, not the person
- Provide specific, actionable feedback
- Be encouraging while pointing out areas for improvement
- Ask questions to understand different approaches

### Issue Discussions
- Stay on topic and be constructive
- Provide clear, detailed information
- Be patient with responses and follow-ups
- Help triage and organize issues when possible

### Feature Discussions
- Consider the broader community impact
- Respect different use cases and workflows
- Be open to alternative solutions
- Think about accessibility and inclusivity

### Music-Related Discussions
- Respect different musical tastes and genres
- Avoid gatekeeping or elitism about music styles
- Credit artists and sources appropriately
- Discuss music industry topics respectfully

## üõ°Ô∏è Enforcement

### Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

1. **GitHub Issues**: For public code-related conduct issues
2. **Direct Contact**: Email repository maintainers for sensitive matters
3. **GitHub's reporting tools**: For serious violations

### Information to Include

When reporting, please provide:
- **What happened**: Clear description of the incident
- **When and where**: Time and location of the incident
- **Who was involved**: Usernames or identifiers (if safe to share)
- **Evidence**: Screenshots, links, or other supporting material
- **Impact**: How the behavior affected you or others

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: Reports will be acknowledged within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: Thorough review of the incident and context
3. **Decision**: Determination of appropriate response measures
4. **Communication**: Follow-up with reporter and any necessary actions
5. **Documentation**: Record keeping for pattern recognition

### Enforcement Actions

Depending on the severity and context, enforcement actions may include:

- **Warning**: Private conversation about behavior expectations
- **Temporary suspension**: Brief restriction from community participation
- **Permanent ban**: Complete removal from community spaces
- **Public clarification**: Clear community statement about expectations
- **Mediation**: Facilitated conversation between parties

### Appeals Process

If you disagree with an enforcement action:
1. Contact repository maintainers within 30 days
2. Provide additional context or information
3. Request review by different community members
4. Accept final decision after review process

## üå± Building a Better Community

### For Contributors
- **Read the room**: Understand the context and tone of discussions
- **Assume good intentions**: Give others the benefit of the doubt
- **Ask for clarification**: When something is unclear, ask questions
- **Learn from mistakes**: Use feedback as an opportunity to grow
- **Help newcomers**: Share your knowledge and experience generously

### For Maintainers
- **Lead by example**: Model the behavior you want to see
- **Be consistent**: Apply standards fairly across all interactions
- **Communicate clearly**: Explain decisions and provide context
- **Stay approachable**: Be available for questions and concerns
- **Support the community**: Foster an environment where everyone can thrive

### For Music Creators
- **Respect copyright**: Only share content you have rights to use
- **Credit properly**: Always acknowledge original creators and collaborators
- **Be professional**: Maintain professionalism in business discussions
- **Support others**: Promote and encourage fellow artists
- **Share responsibly**: Consider the impact of what you share
